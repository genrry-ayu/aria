— About

I'm Aria. I design
with care, write with
curiosity.

I grew up in Hangzhou, studied interaction design at Zhejiang University, and now live in Beijing. My career took me from a small YC-backed startup in San Francisco to Alibaba, and now to ByteDance — where I work on Feishu Bitable, a product used by millions.

I believe the best design comes from deep empathy with the people who use it. My approach is grounded in research, refined through prototyping, and validated by real users. I also think designers should write — it clarifies thinking and builds bridges with engineering.
