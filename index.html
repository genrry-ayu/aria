On the Invisible Craft of AI Interfaces

The best AI interactions don't feel like talking to a machine. They feel like extension — of thought, of intention. But getting there requires a kind of restraint that's counterintuitive for product designers. We're so used to making things visible, legible, explicit. But good AI is invisible. It anticipates. It stays out of the way.
